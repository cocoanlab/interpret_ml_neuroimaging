The ‘derivatives’ folder includes:
- ‘trial_images’ folder: single trial beta maps for each subject and ‘heat’ and rejection condition
- ‘contrast_images’ folder: contrast images for each subject and ‘heat’, ‘warmth’, ‘friend’, and ‘rejection’ conditions 
- ‘nuisance.mat’ – example nuisance variable (head motion parameters roll, pitch, yaw, x, y, z) matrix for each subject and trial
- ‘mini_example_social_physical_masked_cross.hdf5.zip’
